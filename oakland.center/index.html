Oakland Center for the Arts

Community theater in Warren with a fresh home in the gold-dome church on Market Street.

Oakland Center for the Arts is based at 280 E. Market St., Warren, OH 44481, in the former First United Church of Christ building. The theater is building toward a season of plays, musicals, auditions, and youth opportunities, and recent updates describe it as Warren's community theater and arts center.
