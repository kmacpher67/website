Story of the Polish BBQ Pepper

A Polish Pepper Story of Love & Flavor!

It all began with two young brothers who could never quite say dziadek — the Polish word for “grandfather.” Their playful attempts came out as “Adzi,” and the name stuck. Over time, it became a family nickname for the man whose kitchen lessons shaped generations.

“Adzi” was more than a name — it was the spirit of family, hard work, and joy. In his small Polish kitchen, Grandpa taught them the old ways: how to grow, pickle, and spice peppers with patience and pride. Each batch carried stories of laughter, long summer afternoons, and the belief that food made with love brings people together.

Centuries ago, Poland’s trade routes with the East filled its markets with exotic spices. Those bold flavors became part of our heritage — and they live on in every jar of Adzi’s Polish BBQ Peppers.

Today, our family continues that tradition, blending time-honored recipes with the same warmth and care that Grandpa “Adzi” shared. These peppers are more than just a taste of spice — they’re a taste of family, of heritage, and of the simple, magical moments that connect us all.

From our family to yours — we hope you enjoy Adzi’s Peppers as much as we’ve enjoyed sharing them for generations.
