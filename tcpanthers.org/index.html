Be Fit

Children learn what the see. Exercise and hardwork are life long lessons that start in a great Youth Sports program like the Trumbull County Panthers.

Join Club

Any child from the City of Warren, Ohio and surrounding area can join the Little Panthers Program. Ages 6&7, 8&9, 10&11

Age Based

There are 3 teams: based on maximum age limits 6-7, 8-9, & 10-11 year olds.

Have Fun

Having fun and participation. Youth Sports activities for Cheerleaders and tackle football helps develops childern into healthy adults.

Competition

Competition allows youth participants to learn respect and hardwork.
