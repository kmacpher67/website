Our Mission

At Custom Coated Glass, our mission is to deliver innovative, high-quality glass coating solutions. Specializing in IRR (Infra Red Reflective) and advanced conductive coatings, we transform large and custom-sized sheets of BPS borosilicate and non-standard soda lime glass for a variety of applications – from architectural and interior design projects to industrial and ad-tech marketing installations.

Our Services

We collaborate with our clients to apply either your custom coating designs or our in-house advanced formulations. Our comprehensive service offerings include:

  • High-Quality IRR Infra Red Reflective Coatings
  • Conductive Coatings for Enhanced Electrical Performance
  • Custom Coatings for Architectural, Industrial, and Commercial Applications
  • Advanced Semiconductor Coatings for Privacy and Ad-Tech Solutions

Whether you’re an architect, interior designer, or marketing professional, our innovative glass solutions are designed to impress and perform.
