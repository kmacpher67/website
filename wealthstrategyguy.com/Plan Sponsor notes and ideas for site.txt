SIMPLE IRA Plans
A streamlined option for smaller businesses looking for ease and predictability.

- Lower administrative requirements

- Mandatory employer contributions

- Limited customization compared to 401(k)s

Best for: Small teams prioritizing simplicity over flexibility.

SEP IRA Plans
A contribution-focused plan primarily for owners and small businesses.

- Employer-only contributions

- Flexible contribution levels year to year

- Minimal administrative burden

Best for: Self-employed individuals or businesses with few employees.

Cash Balance Plans (Advanced Strategy)
A powerful tool for high-income business owners looking to accelerate retirement savings.

- Significantly higher contribution limits than traditional plans

- Often paired with a 401(k) for maximum impact

Best for: Owners and professionals with strong, consistent cash flow.

Advanced Contribution & Roth Strategies
This is where thoughtful plan design creates real leverage.

Roth Contributions (Across Plans)
Many plans can incorporate Roth features, allowing participants to:

- Contribute after-tax dollars

- Grow assets tax-free

- Withdraw tax-free in retirement (if qualified)

Why it matters: Adds tax diversification and long-term planning flexibility.
--------------------------------------------------------

box
----------------------------------------------------------------
Backdoor Roth IRA Strategy
For high-income individuals who exceed Roth IRA income limits.

- Contribute to a non-deductible IRA

- Convert to a Roth IRA

Key consideration: Requires careful coordination to avoid unintended tax consequences (pro-rata rule).

- Mega Backdoor Roth (Within 401(k) Plans) One of the most underutilized strategies available.

- Allows for after-tax contributions beyond standard limits

- Converts those contributions into Roth (inside the plan or via rollover)

- Can dramatically increase tax-free retirement assets

Requires:

- Proper 401(k) plan design

- After-tax contribution feature

- In-service distribution or in-plan conversion capability

Best for: High-income earners and owners looking to maximize tax-advantaged savings.

The Hidden Risks Plan Sponsors Face
Many plans are set up once—and then left on autopilot.

That’s where problems develop.

Common issues include:

- Excessive or hidden fees

- Outdated or underperforming investment menus

- Lack of documented fiduciary process

- Poor employee engagement

- Compliance gaps

Without proper oversight, these can create both financial inefficiencies and legal exposure.
