During Pregnancy

Hands On personal support and answers.
As Your Doula, I can help you discuss your options with you so you can make informed decisions. My coaching and support can also provide massaging and relaxation techniques for labor.

Birthing Process

As your Doula, I can help you take charge of your pregnancy health or just be there to guide. They can also suggest ways to ease the stress and pain of labor.

Personalized Childbirth Education

I meet with you and your partner and have a conversation about you and your life plans. We look at: Exploring your Values, Judgements, and Preferences, Nutrition, Strengthening your Birthing Team, Relaxing Massage, Partner Support, Positions and Movements for Labor Progress, Interventions, Pushing, finalizing your Birth Preferences, Hospital Policy and Practice, Be your Best Advocate. Newborn Characteristics and Care, Breastfeeding/Lactation, Postpartum Self Care.

Lactation Support & Postpartum Followup

Emotional support and optional on-site care As your Doula, I can go the extra listen to your new parent concerns and thoughts, and celebrate their happiness together after a positive birth. They can also help identify early signs of depression and provide resources for getting help. During Postpartum I will provide families information and support on infant feeding, emotional and physical recovery from childbirth, infant soothing, and coping skills for new parents.
