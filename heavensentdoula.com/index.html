During Pregnancy

Hands-on personal support and answers. As your Doula, I can help you discuss your options so you can make informed decisions. My coaching and support can also provide massaging and relaxation techniques for labor.

Birthing Process

As your Doula, I can help you take charge of your pregnancy health or just be there to guide. I can also suggest ways to ease the stress and pain of labor.

Personalized Childbirth Education

I meet with you and your partner to discuss your life plans. Topics include nutrition, relaxation techniques, labor positions, newborn care, breastfeeding, and postpartum self-care.

Lactation Support & Postpartum Followup

Emotional support and optional on-site care. I provide families with information on infant feeding, emotional and physical recovery from childbirth, infant soothing, and coping skills for new parents.
