Ocean Dark Springs

A JP2M underwater strategy adventure where fallen Elders guard corrupted regions of the ocean — and victory comes from outsmarting them, not simply overpowering them.

Story Gameplay Boss Elders Concept Art

Story

Long ago, the Elders maintained balance between ocean life, currents, ancient ruins, and the surface world. One brilliant but rejected Elder was denied a place among the ruling circle. Angry and humiliated, he released a psychic corruption into the sea to force the other Elders into doing terrible things.

The infected Elders are not mindless monsters. They are former protectors — powerful, strategic, territorial, and tragic. The player is an ancient deep-ocean Elder who avoided infection by living far below the reach of the outbreak.

The game begins in the abyss. The player must rise through the ocean, complete each region, enter its boss arena, and defeat or cleanse twelve fallen Elders before confronting the rejected Elder who broke the ocean.

Gameplay Direction

Tone: mysterious, strategic, adventurous, and exciting — less horror, less “deadly beast,” more tactical challenge against corrupted former Elders.

World Structure

Act 1: The Deep

Bioluminescent trenches, geothermal vents, ancient ruins, and deep coral temples. The player awakens here as the uninfected Elder.

Act 2: Midwater Collapse

Flooded cities, wrecked temples, current mazes, and kelp labyrinths. More puzzle-heavy, with complex boss arenas.

Act 3: Surface Fringe

Two adjacent areas partly above water: the Broken Shore and the Sunken Citadel. Tide timing, stealth routes, and vertical traversal matter.

Final Act

The corrupted Elder sanctuary and the rejected Elder throne chamber. The truth of the outbreak is revealed.

The 12 Fallen Elders

Each boss is a corrupted former Elder with a unique arena, strategy, and ability reward. The goal is not just to fight — it is to learn the Elder’s pattern, use the environment, and restore balance.

Ocean Dark Spring

A corrupted root-and-current Elder. Uses terrain hazards, vines, and pressure bursts. First major boss.

The Split Mind

A former wisdom Elder whose mind fractured into illusions. The arena becomes a strategy puzzle of real vs. false targets.

Abyss Harvester

A tactical ambush Elder. Players predict tail strikes, use terrain traps, and redirect currents to expose weak points.

Tide Marshal

An armored combat Elder with shield phases, pattern attacks, and arena control mechanics.

Rift Drake Elder

A high-mobility late-game Elder. Uses storm currents, vertical movement, and phase-shifting attacks.

The Player Elder

The deep-ocean Elder who avoided the infection. Uses sonar, dash movement, pressure shields, and cleansing abilities.

Gameplay HUD Mockup Direction

The trailer and prototype should look like a real action-adventure game: third-person camera, readable HUD, ability wheel, sonar minimap, objective markers, and boss health bars.

  • Health / Elder Energy: tracks player survival and special ability power.
  • Sonar Minimap: reveals hidden paths, weak points, and puzzle objects.
  • Ability Wheel: dash, shield, grapple, pulse, vortex, and cleanse.
  • Boss Bar: shows each fallen Elder’s corruption state instead of just health.
  • Objective Prompts: “Redirect current,” “Expose weak point,” “Cleanse node.”
