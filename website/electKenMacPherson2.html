Citizens Initiative: Repeal the Pay Raises

In the closing days of 2023, Warren’s part-time city council pushed through a law doubling their own salaries. They even included automatic pay raises tied to city workers. Here’s how we fix this travesty with a people’s petition:

  • Set city council pay to a reasonable amount instead of the doubled amount.
  • Repeal the automatic pay raise system for politicians.
  • Require voter approval for any future pay raises.

We need over 1,000 signatures by July 31st, 2025, to get this initiative on the ballot for November 4th, 2025. Let’s take back our city government!
