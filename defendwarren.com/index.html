About the Podcast

Hosted by James Guy, Jr., the Defend Warren podcast is dedicated to the economic growth and development of the City of Warren, Ohio. We believe in positivity and capturing real stories of those who live in and love Warren. Personal interviews highlight the good happening in and around the community. Negativity not allowed!
