About Us

At Unity Specialty Pharmacy, we focus on your healthcare so you can focus on being healthy. We are committed to providing affordable access to essential medications and personalized care for every patient. Our team forms strategic partnerships to help reduce prescription costs and ensure that no one has to sacrifice their health due to financial limitations. We proudly advocate for the health and well-being of all communities, including the LGBTQIA+ community, and strive to make specialty pharmacy services accessible to everyone.

Our Services

  • Specialty Pharmacy Services: Prevention, treatment, and management of complex conditions, including PrEP & HIV care, gender-affirming hormone therapy, and more.
  • Vaccinations: COVID-19, flu, DTaP, hepatitis, and other essential vaccines. Walk-ins and appointments available.
  • Personal Consultations: Schedule an appointment for one-on-one support and guidance from our expert pharmacists.
  • Patient Resources: Stay informed with up-to-date healthcare resources and support for your medication and wellness needs.
  • Insurance Assistance: We work with a wide range of insurance providers to help you access the medications you need.
